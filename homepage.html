OUR BUSINESS

Vilakazi Solutions is committed to producing and selling pure ice cream and bakery products, and to branch out to other businesses in future. The objective is simple and straight forward which is to deliver a quality product in a consistent, favourable and timely manner in order to have customers return again for another satisfying flavoursome treat while at the same time allowing us to earn a reasonable return on the initial investment.

In order to earn a reasonable return on the investment, along with the ability to repay debt, strict cost-control measures will be implemented. These measures will include, among others, proper and prudent purchasing practices, maximization of product distribution through strict adherence to weights, amounts and recipes (portion control), effective utilization of personnel, and the constant search for ways to reduce the cost of sales of our products without sacrificing quality and service. In summary, the principals are committed to ensuring that this operation is successful. The Vilakazi ice cream will use an ice cream stall machine with three flavours, pure vanilla, strawberry and vanilla, and chocolate and vanilla. The Digestics will supply Vilakazi with ice cream powders that will be mixed with water inside the machine to produce a tasty ice cream.
