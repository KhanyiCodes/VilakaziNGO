Our Work

Vilakazi Solutions is committed to produce and selling pure ice cream and bakery products. The objective is simple and straight forward which is to deliver a quality product in a consistent, favourable and timely manner in order to have customers return again for another satisfying flavoursome treat while at the same time allowing us to earn a reasonable return on the initial investment.


The Vilakazi Solutions use an ice cream stall machine with three flavours, pure vanilla, strawberry and vanilla, and chocolate and vanilla. The Digestics will supply Milky Way with ice cream powders that will be mixed with water inside the machine to produce a tasty ice cream. On the bakery side, we use different baking machines to produce tasty cakes, bunds, muffins an bread.
